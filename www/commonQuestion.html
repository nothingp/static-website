How to distinguish cartridge-with-hole and cartridge-without-hole

Cartridge with hole

Cartridge without hole

There are two types of Brother’s cartridges. Judging from appearance, they can be named “hole-type” cartridge and “non-hole-type” cartridge. As the picture shown, a rectangle hole of the cartridge appearing in red square is “hole-type” cartridge; while no hole appearing in red square is “non-hole-type” cartridge. According to this noticeable feature, two types of cartridges can be differentiated easily. In fact, there are three portions in the cartridge detected by electrical signal, which are respectively first signal blocking portion, second signal blocking portion, third signal blocking portion. The hole is in the second signal blocking portion. When the width of the second signal blocking portion is narrow, it can be seen as a “hole-type” cartridge; while the width of the second signal blocking portion is wide, it can be seen as a “non-hole-type” cartridge.
